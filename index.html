My Reason and Goals for Joining ALT_SCHOOL

Reason

I joined ALT_SCHOOL School of Engineering to pursue the Cloud Engineering course. Initially, it was mere curiosity that drew me in. However, I quickly realized that there was a significant gap in the field that needed to be filled. This realization prompted me to take the initiative to train and develop the necessary skills to address this gap.

After extensive research into cloud engineering, my curiosity evolved into a deep-seated desire to learn and excel in this area. Nigeria is on the cusp of a massive technological breakthrough, with even the smallest businesses moving online and targeting foreign markets. There is a pressing need for indigenous cloud engineers who understand and can navigate the economic climate of our country and continent, and who are dedicated to helping startups, organizations, businesses, and even the government scale and grow with secure and well-designed cloud infrastructures.

My goal is to become one of those engineers, contributing to the growth and success of businesses in Nigeria and beyond.

My Goals

My primary goal is to acquire advanced technical skills that will enable me to contribute effectively to software development projects. I aim to learn the latest technologies, frameworks, and methodologies used in the industry to stay ahead of the curve and be well-prepared for future challenges.

Additionally, I hope to network with like-minded individuals and professionals, collaborate on innovative projects, and gain practical experience through hands-on learning opportunities. These experiences will not only enrich my knowledge but also help me build a robust professional portfolio.
